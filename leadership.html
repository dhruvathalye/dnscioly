2025–2026 Officer Positions

Our Leadership

The officers who lead DNSO's six teams, manage operations, and keep our club running strong.

📣 All positions are open — any current member can run!

Officer Positions

👑

Co-Presidents
(2 positions)

______________________
______________________
⚠ Juniors only

Leads the student body by running club-wide meetings and making important decisions in collaboration with the head coach and Vice President. Works with the head coach, VP, and Event Advisor to build rosters for all 6 teams. Coordinates with the Treasurer to ensure funds are spent efficiently, with the Secretary to maintain ASB compliance, and with the Technology Coordinator to keep the website and information database up to date.

Club Meetings Roster Building Budget Oversight ASB Compliance Website Coordination
🤝

Vice President

______________________
⚠ Sophomores only

Leads club-wide meetings when the Presidents are absent and helps make key decisions alongside the Presidents and head coach. Works with the head coach, Presidents, and Event Advisor to build rosters for all 6 teams. Also responsible for coordinating the Event Advisor and Media Advisor.

Meeting Leadership Roster Building Advisor Coordination
💰

Treasurer

______________________

Responsible for all financial tasks of the club: balancing the budget, maintaining the bank account, calculating a reasonable registration fee, placing T-shirt orders, and filling out reimbursement forms for coaches and parent helpers. Also responsible for reaching out to corporations for potential sponsorship opportunities.

Budget Bank Account Registration Fees T-Shirt Orders Reimbursements Sponsorships
📋

Secretary / Publicity

______________________

In charge of the media team, managing items such as posters, flyers, club advertising, and T-shirts — organizing the process rather than designing everything personally. Keeps club minutes, takes notes during club-wide meetings, and ensures the club meets all ASB regulations.

Media Team Posters & Flyers Club Minutes ASB Regulations Advertising
💻

Technology Coordinator

______________________
💡 Coding experience recommended

In charge of maintaining the club website and a functional database of event information. Helps build a long-term knowledge base for Del Norte — logging event-specific details year over year (e.g., question formats, station layouts, which schools excel at which events, how to source building materials and help) so that students and coaches always have maximum information going forward.

Website Maintenance Event Database Knowledge Base Historical Logs
🔧

Building Event Advisor

______________________
⚠ Must have competed in 1+ building event

Oversees the progress of all building events. Fully understands all applicable Science Olympiad rules for building events and ensures all participants follow them and stay within guidelines. Should be familiar with building events from prior experience. Assists coaches and students in procuring materials and funds, deciding on build plans, and executing the build process.

Build Oversight Rules Compliance Materials Procurement Build Execution Funding
